About WHO Growth Charts

These charts are based on the World Health Organization standards for healthy breastfed babies. They show how your baby's growth compares to babies of the same age and gender worldwide.

Remember

Every baby grows at their own pace! These charts are guidelines. Trust your instincts and consult your pediatrician if you have concerns.
